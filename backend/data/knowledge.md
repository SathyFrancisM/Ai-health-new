# MediGuide Knowledge Base: Traditional Indian Home Remedies

## Introduction
This document contains a curated list of traditional Indian home remedies (Ayurveda/Patti Vaithiyam) for common health issues.

---

## 1. Cough (Khansi / Irumal)
*   **Symptoms:** Throat irritation, dry cough, wet cough, congestion.
*   **Remedy 1: Ginger & Honey (Adrak aur Shahad)**
    *   **Ingredients:** Fresh Ginger juice (1 tsp), Raw Honey (1 tbsp).
    *   **Instructions:** Mix ginger juice with honey. Consume twice a day, especially before bedtime for a dry cough.
*   **Remedy 2: Turmeric Milk (Haldi Doodh)**
    *   **Ingredients:** Milk (1 glass), Turmeric powder (1/4 tsp), Black pepper (pinch).
    *   **Instructions:** Boil milk with turmeric and pepper. Drink warm before bed to soothe the throat and boost immunity.

---

## 2. Fever (Bukhaar / Kaichal)
*   **Symptoms:** High body temperature, shivering, body ache.
*   **Remedy 1: Tulsi Tea (Basil Water)**
    *   **Ingredients:** Tulsi (Holy Basil) leaves (10-12), Water (2 cups).
    *   **Instructions:** Boil tulsi leaves in water until it reduces to one cup. Drink warm 2-3 times a day.
*   **Remedy 2: Giloy Decoction**
    *   **Ingredients:** Giloy stem or juice (2 tbsp), Water.
    *   **Instructions:** Mix Giloy juice with water. Giloy is known as "Amrita" for its fever-lowering and immunity-boosting properties.

---

## 3. Stomach Issues (Digestion / Acidity)
*   **Symptoms:** Stomach ache, bloating, gas, indigestion.
*   **Remedy 1: Ajwain & Black Salt**
    *   **Ingredients:** Ajwain (Carom seeds) (1/2 tsp), Black Salt (1/4 tsp).
    *   **Instructions:** Swallow the mixture with warm water for instant relief from gas and bloating.
*   **Remedy 2: Ginger Water**
    *   **Ingredients:** Sliced ginger (1 inch), Water (1 cup).
    *   **Instructions:** Boil ginger in water for 5 minutes. Sip slowly after meals to aid digestion.

---

## 4. Headache (Sir Dard / Thalai Vali)
*   **Symptoms:** Pain in temples, migraine, tension headache.
*   **Remedy 1: Peppermint / Eucalyptus Oil**
    *   **Ingredients:** Peppermint oil or Eucalyptus oil.
    *   **Instructions:** Gently massage a drop of oil onto the temples and forehead. Avoid contact with eyes.
*   **Remedy 2: Cinnamon Paste**
    *   **Ingredients:** Cinnamon powder, Water.
    *   **Instructions:** Mix cinnamon powder with a few drops of water to make a paste. Apply on the forehead and wash off after 15 minutes.

---

## 5. Common Cold (Sardi / Jaladosham)
*   **Symptoms:** Running nose, sneezing, nasal congestion.
*   **Remedy 1: Steam Inhalation**
    *   **Ingredients:** Hot water, Eucalyptus oil or Carom leaves.
    *   **Instructions:** Inhale steam from a bowl of hot water for 5-10 minutes. Cover your head with a towel.
*   **Remedy 2: Pepper Soup (Milagu Rasam)**
    *   **Ingredients:** Black pepper, Cumin, Garlic, Tamarind water.
    *   **Instructions:** Drink hot pepper soup (rasam) to clear sinus congestion and clear the throat.

---

## Safety & Allergies
> [!CAUTION]
> Always check for allergies! If you are allergic to ginger, turmeric, or any other spice, please avoid that remedy.
> These are home remedies for mild symptoms. If symptoms persist for more than 3 days, consult a qualified medical professional.
